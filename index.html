About the model

We plan on creating an application that cares of most personal needs by automating access and using AI to generate options and send based on user request. It will be voice-enabled with potential to upgrade to technical tasks including but not limited to making investments based on popular tweets and sending business emails.

What next Future Scope Automating personal tasks that would otherwise stand undone one's TODO list, our app can be a staple requirement in every future phone. As more operations get automated and optimized, the opportunities of this app being an inbuilt one brings a huge upside with AI making more radical advances in our personal life.
